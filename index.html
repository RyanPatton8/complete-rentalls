Who We Are

Complete RentAlls was established in 1968 and was located on Colborne St in a 1500 sq ft location and offered 150 pieces of equipment for rental. As the business began to grow and flourish it was evident that more space was needed, at this point another move to its present day location was necessary. Today Complete Rent-Alls is located at 77 Memorial Ave in a 8,400 sq ft location with more than 1,600 pieces of equipment for rental, our present day staff is comprised of 5 full time and 3 part time employees.

As a FIVE-YEAR recipient of the Orillia Readers Choice Award for Favourite Party Supplies. We make sure we have what you need for all your special events. View and print off our Party list and be sure to fax it or bring it in to plan your next event, after the selections have been made the Complete Rent Alls Team will deliver the products. Come see us at 77 Memorial Ave Monday – Saturday for all your rental needs.

At Complete RentAlls we believe in caring the best product available in the industry to help serve you (our customer) better and more efficiently. We carry brand name products and equipment that we rent for your home improvement or construction trade needs.
